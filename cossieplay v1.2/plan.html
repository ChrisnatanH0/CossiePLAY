Goals

The purpose of this CossiePLAY website is to promote their Cosplay shop to a wider range of audiences, specifically younger audiences, and to increase the sales of costumes and party sign-ups.

Success Evaluation

Success of this site will be attributed with the increased number of costumes purchased as well as the number of people signing up for parties. The number of sales and sign-ups will be compared with the numbers from a month ago.

Target Audience

The main target audiences are young people, from high-schoolers to young adults. In order to attract younger audiences, this site will be designed with bright simplistic colors as well as images of young cosplayers portraying popular icons.
